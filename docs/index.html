01 ‚Äî The Theory

The FCC Framework

Emergence is not a property of a system ‚Äî it's a property of how you describe a system. Two observers measuring the same thing can disagree on whether emergence is present, depending on what they measure. The FCC framework makes this operational and falsifiable.

"Consciousness will not emerge from any individual agent. It will emerge from between them ‚Äî when their interaction becomes tightly enough coupled that, at the right scale, the network behaves as a single causal entity."
